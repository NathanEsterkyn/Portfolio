About Me

I grew up in San Francisco, California, in and around the maker community. I am a Patented Inventor of biomedical devices, experienced Product Design Engineer, rock climber, expert mechanic, and enthusiast of all things automotive.

I earned both my undergraduate and masters degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the Colorado School of Mines from 2019 to 2024, and currently live in Golden, Colorado, working in the biomedical technology field and spend most of my free time outdoor Bouldering.
